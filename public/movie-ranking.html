The Best Movies According to Anne

My top 3 favorite movies

The Emperor's New Groove

This is my absolute favorite movie, hands down. The casting is brilliant, the humor is reminiscent of Monty Python while still having a lot of heart, and there are just so many quotable lines.

The Usual Suspects

A great movie that lands somewhere between a murder mystery and a heist movie. The plot twist at the end is executed brilliantly.

Clue

If you need me to elaborate on why this is so good, you clearly haven't seen the movie (and should probably fix that). The writing is superb, and the cast is hilarious. Tim Curry and Madeline Khan are particular standouts, and I still quote the "Flames... Flames, on the side of my face..." line to this day.
